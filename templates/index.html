About This Calculator

Sidereal Whole Sign System

This calculator uses the sidereal zodiac for planet positions, which accounts for the precession of the equinoxes and provides more astronomically accurate positions compared to the tropical system.

For house calculations, we use the whole sign system, one of the oldest house systems where each house corresponds to a full sign of the zodiac, starting with the rising sign (ascendant) as the 1st house.

Features
  • Accurate planetary positions with retrograde status
  • Nakshatra (lunar mansion) positions for each planet
  • Whole sign house cusps with interpretations
  • Detailed visual chart representation
  • Western-style circular chart diagram
